Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we handle your information at Party Birthday Studio.

We don't sell your information. We share it only with trusted providers who help us run the site and send emails (e.g., our site host and email service).

We keep your email until you unsubscribe or until we remove inactive subscribers.

Our site and emails are not for children under 13 (or higher local age where required).
